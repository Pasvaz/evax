## Survival Mechanics
### Q&A
#### why do i need to collect resources?
you need to collect resources to keep your hunger up by eating the resources you dont starve because when you starve you slowly lose health eventually leading to your demise.
you need to collect resources so you can craft, sell them and make your score go up.

#### why do i need money?
you need money so you can buy things from merchants.

#### why do i get money when i collect?
you get money when you collect resources because that is one of the only two ways of getting money
you get money when you collect resources because if you are on low hunger and dont want to sell resources because you want to preserve resources for hunger you can still get a little money.

#### why can i buy things and why?
you can buy things to help you better advance through the game to discover new lands, new creatures and discover how you got here and what are the secrets of this world.

#### why do i get a score?
score is practically exp. in the future i will add new things you can unlock through spending exp. 
#### why do i craft?
you craft so you can get more score and survive harsh new enviroments by crafting things and items eg.
* a gas mask for wastelands
* weapons to kill and hunt food so you dont starve.
* speed boosters to escape dangerous predators
* magic items to summon animals or view them to discover their behavioral patterns and learn about the animals their models are based off of
